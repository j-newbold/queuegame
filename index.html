Instructions

Click "Initialize" to set up the simulation. The middle area will be populated with projects at varying stages of completion. Your job is to find the number of projects that maximizes your throughput (number of projects completed per month). When you press "Start/Stop", the simulation will run, and projects will complete. Too many active projects will strain your company's resources, and too few will not utilize enough of them. Click on a project that's running to pause it, putting it into the left area. Click on a paused project to put it back into the active projects area. If you don't have any paused projects, you can add a new project with the "Add a Project" button.
